Our History

TechCon began over a decade ago as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts and has since grown into one of the most influential technology conferences in the world. Over the years, it has become a hub for showcasing groundbreaking innovations and fostering collaboration among leaders in the industry.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to inspire and empower individuals and organizations to harness technology for positive global change. We strive to create a platform where ideas are shared, innovations are celebrated, and future leaders are empowered to shape the world.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Renowned as one of the first computer programmers, Dr. Lovelace’s visionary insights have inspired generations of technologists.

Alan Turing

A pioneer of computer science and artificial intelligence, Alan Turing’s contributions continue to shape modern computing.

Grace Hopper

Grace Hopper, a trailblazer in programming languages, laid the foundation for modern software development and inspired innovation across the tech industry.
